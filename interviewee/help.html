Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I schedule an interview?

To schedule an interview, navigate to the scheduling section, select the candidate and interviewer, and choose a suitable date and time.

How do I track the progress of a candidate?

You can track the progress of a candidate by going to the progress tracking section and viewing their current status in the interview process.

How do I generate reports?

To generate reports, go to the reports section, select the type of report you need, and customize the parameters as required.
